# Plan Upgrade and Downgrade Rules

## Summary

Customers may request to upgrade or downgrade their plan. Support agents should confirm the current plan, desired plan, billing cycle, renewal date, and whether the change should take effect immediately or at the next renewal.

## Standard Handling

Upgrades may change available features and billing immediately depending on the product configuration. Downgrades may remove access to premium features, usage limits, seats, or integrations.

Agents should not promise exact pricing changes without checking the account and current billing configuration.

## Customer-Safe Wording

"I can help review your plan change options. Before confirming the change, I will check the current plan, billing cycle, and any feature or usage impact."

## Escalation

Escalate enterprise plan changes, contract customers, custom pricing, annual prepaid plans, or requests involving refunds or credits.
