# Data Privacy Request Handling

## Summary

Requests involving personal data, account deletion, data export, privacy rights, GDPR, CCPA, or similar regulations must be handled carefully and may require verification or escalation.

## Standard Handling

Support agents should identify the request type: data access, data export, data correction, account deletion, consent withdrawal, or privacy complaint.

Agents must verify the requester's identity before discussing or changing personal data. Do not share account details, personal information, or internal privacy procedures in a public or unverified channel.

## Customer-Safe Wording

"I can help start that privacy request. For security, we may need to verify your identity before processing or discussing account-specific personal data."

## Escalation

Escalate all legal, regulatory, law enforcement, data deletion, and formal privacy rights requests to the privacy or legal team.
