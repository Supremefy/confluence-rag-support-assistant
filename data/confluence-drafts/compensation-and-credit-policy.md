# Compensation and Credit Policy

## Summary

Support agents must not promise compensation, service credits, free months, discounts, or account adjustments unless the customer clearly qualifies under an approved policy or a supervisor has approved the exception.

## Standard Handling

If a customer requests compensation, agents should collect the account email, order ID, issue timeline, customer impact, and any prior support interactions.

Agents may acknowledge the request and explain that it requires review. Do not state that compensation will be granted until approval is confirmed.

## Customer-Safe Wording

"I understand you are requesting compensation for the impact. I can document the details and submit the request for review, but I cannot confirm the outcome until it has been evaluated."

## Escalation

Escalate all compensation requests involving outages, repeated billing errors, enterprise accounts, public complaints, legal threats, or high-value customers.
