# Trial Extension Policy

## Summary

Trial extensions may be granted in limited situations, such as onboarding delays, technical access issues, or evaluation blockers that were not caused by the customer.

## Standard Handling

Support agents should confirm the customer account, original trial start date, trial end date, product being evaluated, and reason for the extension request.

Agents may not promise a trial extension before checking eligibility. If the customer had technical access issues during the trial, collect error messages, dates, and screenshots if available.

## Customer-Safe Wording

"I can review whether your account is eligible for a trial extension. Please share the reason for the extension request and any details about issues that prevented you from evaluating the service during the original trial period."

## Escalation

Escalate requests from enterprise prospects, partner-led evaluations, or customers asking for more than one trial extension.
