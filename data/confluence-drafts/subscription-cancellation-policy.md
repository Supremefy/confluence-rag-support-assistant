# Subscription Cancellation Policy

## Summary

Customers may request to cancel a subscription at any time. Support agents should confirm the account email, subscription plan, billing cycle, renewal date, and whether the customer is asking for cancellation only or cancellation with a refund.

## Standard Handling

If the customer only wants to stop future renewals, agents may guide them through the cancellation process or submit the cancellation request on their behalf according to the current support workflow.

Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically create a refund. If the customer also requests money back, agents must follow the refund policy and review the purchase date, service usage, and payment status.

## Customer-Safe Wording

"I can help you stop future renewals for this subscription. If you are also requesting a refund for the current billing period, I will need to review the order details and applicable refund policy before confirming the outcome."

## Escalation

Escalate the ticket if the customer is on an enterprise contract, claims they were billed after cancellation, requests compensation, or threatens a chargeback.
