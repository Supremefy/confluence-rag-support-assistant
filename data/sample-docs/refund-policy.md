# Refund Policy

Customers may submit a refund request within 14 days of purchase. Support agents must confirm the order ID, purchase date, product type, and service usage before advising on the outcome.

If the customer has clearly used the service, agents must not promise a full refund. Agents may explain that the request needs to be reviewed based on actual usage and that the customer may be eligible for a partial refund or special approval.

High-value orders, disputed payments, compensation requests, and contract customers must be escalated to a supervisor or the finance team.
